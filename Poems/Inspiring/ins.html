Inspiring Poem 1.

When you left
I know you feels like nothing happened,
But I feel a lot
I can’t eat,
I can’t sleep,
All I was depressed for a couple of months,
Than I realize I have to move on,
And I decided I will love myself more than
Once I loved you. -Anu
