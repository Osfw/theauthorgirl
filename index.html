Welcome To The Girl Author's Nest

We all need some free time to relax our brain, don't we? The form of finding relaxation differs person to person. Besides sleeping, some of us likes to watch movies, some want to play games or people like us like to dive deep into the pages of our favorite books.

In this website we are putting all our imaginations or experiences on the screen. From romance to horror, from inspiring stories to comedy, our writers are providing the best of us. Hope you have a good time here!

About 'The Girl Author'

'The Girl Author' is the reflection of my primary hobby. When it is about expressing feelings, experiences or thoughts, I feel no way is better than writing. I have invested most of my free times writing my experiences or adding wings to my imaginations. Some of my school friends used to read them, didn't realize when a small fan base formed.

So, now walking with the technological world and launched this website hoping it may help produce a little good time for all the story lovers. Luckily we are a team of 4 girls now and trying to produce the best we can.
