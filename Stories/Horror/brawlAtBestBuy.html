At Best Buy?

Outside a Best Buy in Augusta, Ga., on Black Friday 2010, some U.S. Marines were collecting donations for Toys for Tots. Inside the Best Buy, a man was attempting to smuggle a laptop computer out of the store under his jacket.

Staff spotted the shoplifter and confronted him. The man dropped the laptop, brandished a knife and ran out the door. One of the Marines attempted to stop him, and the robber stabbed him in the back. Other Marines and store staff pounced on the shoplifter and held him until police arrived. The wounded Marine eventually recovered.
